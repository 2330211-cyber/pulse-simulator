Interactive Pulse Modulation Simulator

Visualize and experiment with PAM, PWM, PPM and PCM. Adjust amplitude, sampling frequency, waveform type and see the recovered signal — all in your browser.

Tip: Use the "Demodulation" toggle to see the recovered waveform. Use the "PCM" mode to see quantization and SQNR.

Live preview (if supported by your browser). Otherwise click Open Simulator.

Modes

Switch between PAM, PWM, PPM and PCM. PWM is now fully bipolar comparator-based.

Controls

Adjust amplitude, analog frequency, sampling frequency and PCM bits; export PNG/CSV and inspect recovered signal.

For teachers

Use this page in labs or demonstrations. Share the link with students to interactively teach sampling and modulation.

Quick usage

  1. Open the simulator using the big button above.
  2. Choose waveform and modulation type.
  3. Toggle demodulation to compare recovered and original signals.
